## 4. Known Logistics Challenges

### Seasonal Peaks
- **Summer is peak beer season** — Memorial Day through Labor Day drives massive volume spikes
- Super Bowl week is the single biggest delivery event (featured in press)
- Holiday periods (4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving) create surge demand
- Seasonal peaks = **yard congestion, dock bottlenecks, driver wait times**

### Cold Chain Complexity
- Beer is temperature-sensitive — not refrigerated but heat-sensitive
- Freshness is a competitive differentiator (Budweiser "Born On" dating)
- Cold chain for seltzers and ready-to-drink cocktails (growing category)
- Temperature control at dock = dwell time sensitivity

### DSD Complexity
- Three-tier system creates **multiple handoff points** — brewery yard → wholesaler yard → retail
- 600+ wholesalers nationally, each with their own yard operations
- High SKU count (500+ brands) = complex loading/staging/sequencing
- Route trucks loaded nightly for morning delivery = **tight dock scheduling windows**
- Mixed loads (kegs, cases, bottles, cans) = complex trailer configurations

### M&A Integration Legacy
- Years of acquisitions (SABMiller 2016, Grupo Modelo, etc.) created **fragmented systems**
- Different facilities run different processes — standardization is ongoing
- Elito's entire mandate has been reducing this complexity

### Direct Evidence of Yard Management Pain
1. **Sensolus partnership** explicitly targeted "digitize yard management" — AB InBev has acknowledged this as a gap
2. **15% trailer fleet optimization** in 4 months suggests significant prior inefficiency

### Inferred Yard Pain Points
- **12 breweries** each shipping to ~50+ wholesalers = 600+ receiving yards in the network
- **Night loading for morning delivery** = tight dock windows, no room for congestion
- **Seasonal surges** (summer) likely create 30-50% volume spikes at the dock
- **Mixed fleet** (owned + 3PL + wholesaler trucks) = yard visibility nightmare
- **Electric vehicle transition** adds complexity — charging schedules, range planning, dock priority for EVs

### The Sensolus Gap
The Sensolus deployment appears focused on **trailer tracking/IoT** (asset visibility), primarily in Europe. This is **complementary to, not competitive with** YardFlow:
- Sensolus = "Where are my trailers?" (asset tracking)
- YardFlow = "How do I move trailers faster through the dock?" (workflow optimization)
- **YardFlow fills the operational gap** between knowing where a trailer is and actually reducing dock turn time
