## 1. 📧 **FULL SEQUENCE** (4-Step Cold Outreach)

**Use Case:** Multi-touch email campaign for cold prospects

**Generated from:** Account + Persona selection in Studio

### Example: Target Corp → VP of Supply Chain

**Step 1: Initial Email** (Day 0)
```
Subject: Target's DC throughput optimization

Hi [First Name],

I wanted to reach out because I noticed Target is expanding its fulfillment network with 3 new DCs this year.

We work with retailers like you to eliminate yard bottlenecks across multi-facility operations. Our network system has helped similar companies reduce trailer dwell time by 40% and increase cross-dock predictability.

The issue most retailers face: even with great WMS, trailers sit idle because dock scheduling, yard moves, and carrier ETAs aren't connected. It creates a hidden 2-4 hour delay per load.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call next week to discuss how this applies to Target's network?

Best regards
```

**Step 2: Follow-up #1** (Day 3)
```
Subject: Re: Target's DC throughput optimization

Hi [First Name],

Following up on my previous email about yard operations.

I wanted to share a quick insight: most retailers we work with discover that 30-40% of their "WMS delay" is actually yard delay. Trailers wait for dock assignments, yard moves take 2x longer than planned, and drivers arrive but can't get unloaded for hours.

YardFlow solves this by connecting yard operations, dock scheduling, and carrier coordination in one system.

Would Thursday or Friday work for a brief intro call?

Looking forward to connecting
```

**Step 3: Follow-up #2** (Day 7)
```
Subject: [Case study] How Ace Hardware reduced dwell time 38%

Hi [First Name],

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this short.

Ace Hardware had the same challenge: great WMS, but trailers were stuck in the yard for 3-4 hours. After implementing YardFlow, they reduced dwell time to 90 minutes and increased daily throughput by 30%.

The ROI was 4.2x in year one.

I'd love to show you how this could work for Target's network. 10 minutes on your calendar?

Best
```

**Step 4: Breakup Email** (Day 14)
```
Subject: Closing the loop

Hi [First Name],

I haven't heard back, so I'll assume now isn't the right time to discuss yard optimization for Target.

If anything changes or you'd like to revisit this down the road, feel free to reach out. I'll keep an eye on Target's expansion and check back in Q3.

Appreciate your time.

Best regards
```

## 🚀 **HOW TO GENERATE THESE LIVE**

1. **Go to Studio:** https://modex-gtm.vercel.app/studio
2. **Select Account:** Choose from dropdown (e.g., "Target Corp")
3. **Select Persona:** Choose target contact (e.g., "VP of Supply Chain")
4. **Choose Asset Type:**
   - Full Sequence tab → 4-step email campaign
   - One-Pager tab → Sales leave-behind
   - Asset Pack tab → Outreach components
5. **Click Generate:** AI creates personalized content in ~30 seconds
6. **Review & Edit:** Tweak any section before using
7. **Send or Export:** Email directly or copy to clipboard
