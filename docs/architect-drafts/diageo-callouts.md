## Where Diageo is different

Spirits adds a wrinkle: bonded warehouses and excise tax. The yard handoff between bottling and DC has a paper trail with regulatory teeth. Standardizing it isn't just an efficiency move — it's a compliance move.

The wedge: **manufacturing yard orchestration** — DC + production + bonded under one protocol.

## What's at stake

The bonded-yard handoff is the highest-stakes, lowest-tooled part of the spirits supply chain. The first network operator to standardize it captures a moat the category cannot easily catch.
